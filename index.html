PM Kickstart for IT Pros

Short-term Accelerator | 4-Week Intensive Bootcamp

Target Audience

IT professionals who are exploring project management and want a quick, practical foundation before committing fully.

Course Focus
  • Fundamentals of Project Management (Waterfall, Agile, Hybrid)
  • Leading vs. Doing: Making the Mindset Shift
  • Communication Skills & Stakeholder Mapping
  • Hands-on Simulations: Standups, Sprint Reviews, Retros
  • Mini Capstone Project
Format
  • 4 live weekly workshops + 1 mini-project
  • Worksheets, templates, checklists
  • Community support (Slack/Discord)
Outcome
  • Clear roadmap to move from tech to PM
  • Portfolio piece + resume talking points
  • Confidence to pursue PM interviews or next-level coaching
