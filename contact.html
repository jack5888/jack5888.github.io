Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

How long does it take to transition into a PM role?

转变为项目经理角色需要多长时间？

The timeline varies based on your background and career goals. Most of our students begin interviewing for roles within 3-6 months of completing our programs, with many landing offers within 6-9 months of starting their journey.

时间表根据您的背景和职业目标而有所不同。我们的大多数学生在完成项目后的3-6个月内开始面试，许多人在开始旅程后的6-9个月内获得offer。

Do I need prior PM experience to enroll in your programs?

我需要先前的项目经理经验才能参加您的项目吗？

No prior PM experience is required! Our programs are specifically designed for IT professionals looking to transition into PM roles. Many of our most successful students had no formal PM experience before joining.

不需要先前的项目经理经验！我们的项目专为希望转型为项目经理角色的IT专业人士设计。我们许多最成功的学员在加入前没有正式的项目经理经验。

Will I receive certification after completing a program?

完成项目后我会获得证书吗？

Our programs provide a certificate of completion that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume. While we don't offer industry certifications like PMP or CSM directly, our coaching includes guidance on which certifications might benefit your specific career path.

我们的项目提供完成证书，您可以将其添加到您的LinkedIn个人资料和简历中。虽然我们不直接提供PMP或CSM等行业认证，但我们的辅导包括关于哪些认证可能有益于您特定职业道路的指导。

Can I pay for programs in installments?

我可以分期付款吗？

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for all our programs. During your consultation, we can discuss payment options that work best for your situation.

是的！我们为所有项目提供灵活的付款计划。在咨询期间，我们可以讨论最适合您情况的付款选项。
